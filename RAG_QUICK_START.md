# RAG Chatbot - Quick Start Guide

## What is RAG?

**Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)** enhances AI responses by giving the chatbot access to your claims data and documents. Instead of relying only on pre-trained knowledge, the chatbot:

1. **Retrieves** relevant claims and documents from your database
2. **Analyzes** the information for relevance to your question
3. **Generates** personalized responses using both AI and your actual data
