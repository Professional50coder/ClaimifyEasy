## Understanding Results

### ✅ Green = Good
- Insurance covers amount
- Percentage is high (>70%)

### 🟠 Orange = Deductible
- You pay before insurance helps
- Reset each year

### 🔴 Red = Coinsurance  
- Your percentage of costs
- After deductible

### ⚠️ Yellow Warning
- Exceeds policy limit
- Additional costs your responsibility

## Key Terms Explained

### Deductible
```
What: Amount YOU pay before insurance helps
Example: $1,000 deductible
You have a $5,000 surgery
→ You pay $1,000
→ Insurance helps with the rest
→ Resets every year (Jan 1)
```

### Coinsurance
```
What: Your percentage share after deductible
Example: 20% coinsurance
On $9,000 remaining (after $1,000 deductible)
→ You pay: $1,800 (20%)
→ Insurance pays: $7,200 (80%)
```

### Policy Max
```
What: Maximum insurance pays per year
Example: $100,000 max limit
Treatment costs: $150,000
→ Insurance pays: $100,000
→ You pay: $50,000 extra
```

## Tips for Accurate Results

### ✓ DO THIS
- Get actual cost quotes from hospital
- Check your insurance card for exact numbers
- Verify your policy type with insurance company
- Call before treatment to confirm coverage

### ✗ DON'T DO THIS
- Guess on costs
- Assume network/non-network same cost
- Skip pre-authorization if required
- Ignore policy exclusions
